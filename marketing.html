Everything your pet needs in one place

Petora is built for everyday pet care. Track meals, calories, walks, weight changes, medications, vaccines, and vet visits in one clean timeline so you can make better decisions without juggling notes, screenshots, and reminders across multiple apps.

Why Petora

Great pet care is mostly consistency. Petora makes consistency simple by turning daily tasks into quick logs and clear progress views. Whether you are monitoring body condition, checking walk activity, or reviewing nutrition habits, Petora gives you one reliable place to see the full picture.

01 ◦ Nutrition

Log meals in seconds, track daily kcal intake, and keep pantry stock visible so you always know what your pet is eating and how much they are actually getting.

02 ◦ Walks

Record every walk with distance and duration, then review trends over time to understand activity patterns and support healthier routines.

03 ◦ Weight

Small weight changes matter. Petora helps you log weigh-ins consistently and spot trends early, before they become bigger health concerns.

04 ◦ Health records

Keep medications, treatment plans, vaccines, and vet visits organized in one place so important details are always accessible when you need them most.
