Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-01

Petora ("we", "us", "our") provides the Petora mobile app (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and your choices.

1. Data we process

  • Pet and app content you add (profiles, logs, health records, goals, notes).
  • Subscription and entitlement status needed to unlock paid features.
  • Technical data needed for app operation, sync, reliability, and fraud prevention.
  • Location data only if you grant permission (for walk features).

2. How we use data

  • Provide app features (nutrition, walks, weight, health tracking).
  • Sync your data across Apple devices via iCloud/CloudKit.
  • Manage subscriptions and purchase restoration.
  • Maintain app security, support, and legal compliance.

3. Services we use

We use trusted third parties to run the App, including:

  • Apple (App Store, StoreKit, iCloud/CloudKit infrastructure)
  • RevenueCat (subscription infrastructure and entitlement management)

We do not sell personal data.

4. Retention and deletion

We keep data for as long as needed to provide the App and meet legal obligations. You can delete data in-app. If sync is enabled, deletions propagate through iCloud/CloudKit.
