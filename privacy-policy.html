Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-09

This privacy policy applies to the Petora: Pet Care Tracker app (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, created by Barna Martin Kopácsi (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS."

1. Information Collection and Use

Petora stores most user content locally on your device. The Service Provider does not operate a custom backend that directly receives your pet records.

  • Data you enter in the app (for example: pet profiles, logs, health records, goals, and notes) is stored on-device.
  • Optional pet profile photos you choose are stored on-device.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, on-device data (including optional photos) is transmitted via Apple iCloud/CloudKit to sync across your Apple devices.
  • Location data is used only if you grant location permission and use walk-tracking features.
  • Subscription and purchase-related information is processed via Apple StoreKit and RevenueCat to manage paid features and restore purchases.
  • Limited technical information may be processed as needed for app operation, syncing, reliability, and support.

The Application does not require account registration to use core features.

The Application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data or provide core functionality.

2. Third-Party Access

The Application uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. These services may process data as needed to provide app functionality:

  • Apple services (including App Store, StoreKit, iCloud, and CloudKit)
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions and entitlements)

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/

The Service Provider does not sell personal data and does not share personal data with third parties for their own advertising or marketing purposes.

The Service Provider may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law (for example, to comply with legal process)
  • when disclosure is necessary in good faith to protect rights, safety, investigate fraud, or respond to lawful government requests
  • with trusted service providers acting on the Service Provider’s behalf, under obligations to protect data and not use it independently

3. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it, using standard uninstall processes available through your device or app marketplace.

You can also withdraw certain permissions at any time in iOS Settings (for example, Location or Photos access).

4. Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider retains data for as long as needed to provide the Application and comply with legal obligations.

You can delete your app data in-app (Settings > Delete all data). If iCloud/CloudKit sync is enabled, deletions may propagate across your synced devices.

If you would like the Service Provider to delete User Provided Data associated with your use of the Application, contact: barnakopacsi@gmail.com.

The Service Provider will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law.

5. Children

The Application is not directed to children under the age required by local law. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal data from children below that age. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact the Service Provider at barnakopacsi@gmail.com so appropriate action can be taken.

6. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding your information and uses reasonable administrative, technical, and procedural safeguards to protect data processed and maintained by the Application.

7. Medical Disclaimer

Petora provides tracking and informational tools only. It does not provide veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified veterinarian for medical decisions regarding your pet.
